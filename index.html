TENOR TROMBONE

$600

It has a sliding rod mechanism that allows you to change the pitch. It is tuned in B flat, an octave below the trumpet and an octave above the tuba.

BASS TROMBONE

$900

It has a deeper and lower sound than the tenor trombone. It is tuned in B flat just like the tenor trombone, but it has a larger tube, bell and mouthpiece to facilitate playing in the low register.

VALVE TROMBONE

$1200

It is a type of trombone that has a set of valves to vary the pitch instead of (or in addition to) a sliding rod. Although it has been built in sizes from alto to contrabass, it is the B-flat tuned tenor valve trombone that has had the most widespread use.
